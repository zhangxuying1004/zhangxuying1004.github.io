Biography   [back top]

I am currently a first-year Ph.D student in Department of Artificial Intelligence, School of Informatics, Xiamen University, advised by Prof. Rongrong Ji.

My recent research interests are in vision-and-language learning and semi-supervised object detection.

  • 09/2021 -- Now: Ph.D. in Intelligence Science and Technology, Xiamen University, Xiamen, China
  • 05/2020 -- 09/2020: Research Intern, MSRA NLC Group, Beijing, China
  • 09/2018 -- 06/2021: M.S. in Computer Technology, Xiamen University, Xiamen, China
  • 09/2014 -- 06/2018: B.S. in Optoelectronic Information Science and Engineering, China Jiliang University, Zhe Jiang, China
